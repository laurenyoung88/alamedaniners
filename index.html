Alameda Women's Niners Golf Club

The Alameda Women's Niners Golf Club is a group of ladies who play 9 hole rounds every Mon morning starting at 8AM from January thru November. We play fun competitive golf each with a small entry fee and pay out prizes by handicap flight. In addition, we have monthly tournaments and prize celebrations. Come play with us!
